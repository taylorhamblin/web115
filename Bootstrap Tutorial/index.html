How to bake Chocolate Chip Cookies

Home Tutorial



Soft, Chewy, Melt in your mouth goodness. Chocolate Chip Cookies are the most popular cookie in the world. They are great as a snack with milk or even for desert after a long hard day at work. There are so many different ways you can make this delicious cookie. You can add m&m's, peanutbutter, or nuts, make them big, small or even bite size. It all starts off with a very simple and easy recipe that even beginners can get down in one try. Want to learn how to make this amazing cookie?
