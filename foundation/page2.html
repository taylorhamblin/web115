Meet the Artists

Darrian Love

Darrian Love is the founder of Love Tatoo. As a kid, he was interested in art, design and found his love for tattoos early in highschool. He has been tattooing for over 20 years and has worked with some of the most talented tattoo artists out there. Darrian's imagination and skill has brought him to where he is today and he continues to build and grow with his talented employees at Love Tattoo.

Max Wexford

Max Wexford is farily new to the tattoo world. He went to the same highschool as Darian but never thought of being in the tattoo business. He started off by going to art school in downtown Chicago and finished with a masters in Art and Design. Knowing Darrian for years, he was intrduced to the tattoo world and took the skill fairly quickly. He has been on numerous tattoo shows for example, Ink Masters and LA Ink but has decided to stay put at Love Tattoo in Edgewater, Chicago with his good friend Darrian.

Johnny James

Johnny James is from Huntington Beach, California and is someone who takes tattoos to a whole new level. He started off tattooing members in his band in as a joke in highschool but quickly learned that this is what he wanted to do with his career. Johnny has the talent of sketching up outstanding tattoo's within minutes and has a eye for design like no other. He is also part of a band part-time, which is played throughout Love Tattoo on a daily basis.

Skyla Hempfer

Skyla Hempfer is from a small town right outside of Nashville, Tennessee. She came to Darian a few years ago looking for a job as a piercer but was slowly introduced to tattoos as well. She is an outstanding artist and specializes in black and white patterened tattoos but has the ability to take on any type of piercing. Skyla is the only certified piercer at Love Tattoo but loves when she has the time to tattoo artwork on others.
