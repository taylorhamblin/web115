About Us

The Craze Coffee Shope has been around since 2010. This may be our only location at the moment but we plan on growing and branching out to new locations within the next couple years.



We are not just coffee roasters or importers... we are real baristas that carfully hand-craft and brew award winning coffee. Our focus is to bring you fresh & delicious drinks and food from local business's to keep this town running. There is a lot that goes into what we do to bring you nothing but the best. We take pride in our work and we hope you do too!
